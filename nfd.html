About Division

The National Forensics Division (NFD) is the country's central authority for forensic science. We combine advanced instrumentation, secure protocols, and ethical governance to analyze evidence, develop methods, and provide expert testimony. Our mission is to uphold public safety through science-driven, transparent forensic practice.

  • Maintain chain-of-custody standards and accredited lab practices.
  • Develop next-generation forensic methods in DNA, toxicology, digital forensics, and trace analysis.
  • Collaborate with law enforcement, academia, and international partners.
